Band bio

SUBWAY LIGHTS are:
Gary - vocalist/guitarist/programmer
Alex - guitarist/programmer
Jairo - bassist
Nathan - drummer

SUBWAY LIGHTS are a Nashville based rock band whose music is a distinct blend of melodic guitars, synth-pop textures, drums & bass. Their sound draws influence from such creative artists like The Killers, The Bravery, The Cure, David Bowie, The Strokes, Franz Ferdinand, and INXS. The vocals are resounding and melodious, completing the compelling sound of the band.

The band's founding members, Gary and Alex, began writing songs for the yet to be formed band in 2012. Once a set of songs were written and recorded, Gary and Alex decided it was time to put a band together.

Alex's first choice was Jairo. Formerly the bass player for Nashville's former darling punk rock band, The Worsties, Jairo was recruited after the guys got together and discovered they shared a mutual musical vision.

Nathan replaced original drummer, Art, in 2014. Nathan is also a former member of The Worsties. His years of performing with Jairo have had an immediate, positive impact to the band's live sound, as well as in the studio.

The band has released a new, self-titled EP, which can be heard for free on our music page or on our Facebook page.
